Request Account and Data Deletion

This page is provided by Askoxy.AI to allow users to request deletion of their account and associated data in compliance with Google Play policies.

How to request account deletion:

  1. Send an email to support@askoxy.ai from your registered email ID.
  2. Include your registered phone number or user ID (if available) in the email.
  3. Subject of the email: Request to delete my account.
  4. We will process your request within 7 working days and confirm via email once completed.

What data will be deleted:

  • Your account credentials (phone number, user ID)
  • Any order or transaction history linked to the account
  • Stored addresses and preferences

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to our support team at support@askoxy.ai.
